ELECTRICITY MANAGEMENT: MRP extension

Take strategic decision based on your electricity consumption

Purpose

Add mrp features to your electricity app

This extension will enables you to incorporate your production process into your electricity management app. Electricity consumption of a product can now be computed from its bill of material

In order to be able to use the extension, you will have to download/update the electricity management base module. This extension will is free with the purchase of the base module

Usage

The first easy way to track your consumption in odoo

If your product have a bill of material related, a new section will appear in the electricity tab of the product. In this section you will see displayed to total consumption and cost caused by the products in the bom.

If you decide to include it in your product consumption, you will have the choice to include an additional consumption if needed, and the total one will be computed with the correct price
